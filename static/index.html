Install YT Buzz Cookie Helper

1
Open Chrome Extensions
Go to chrome://extensions in your browser
2
Enable Developer Mode
Toggle the "Developer mode" switch in the top-right corner
3
Load Extension
Click "Load unpacked" and select the browser-extension folder from this project
4
Click the Extension Icon
On any page, click the YT Buzz icon and hit "Sync YouTube Cookies Now"

The extension only reads YouTube cookies — it does not track you or access any other data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no subscription, no hidden fees. Just paste a link and download.

Do you store my downloads or data?

No. All downloads are processed in real-time and files are immediately deleted from the server after transfer. We never store, log, or track your activity.

What formats are supported?

You can download videos in MP4 (any resolution from 144p to 4K) or extract audio as MP3. Playlist downloads are also supported.

Why is my download slow?

Download speed depends on your internet connection and the video quality you chose. Higher resolutions (1080p, 4K) are larger files and take longer to process.

Does this work with private or unlisted videos?

No, only publicly available YouTube videos and playlists can be downloaded.

Can I download age-restricted videos?

No, this tool only works with publicly accessible content.
